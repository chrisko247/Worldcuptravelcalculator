Average Cost to Attend the FIFA World Cup 2026

The average cost for international fans attending the FIFA World Cup ranges between $3,000 and $7,000 depending on the host city, number of matches attended, and length of stay.

  • Flights: $400 – $1500 depending on distance
  • Hotels: $150 – $400 per night during the tournament
  • Match tickets: $120 – $600 depending on match stage
  • Food and transportation: $50 – $120 per day
