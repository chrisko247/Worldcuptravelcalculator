How Much Does It Cost to Attend the World Cup 2026?

The cost of attending the FIFA World Cup 2026 depends on several factors, including flights, accommodation, match ticket prices, daily food expenses, and the length of your stay. Fans traveling internationally typically spend between $3,000 and $7,000 for the full World Cup experience.

World Cup 2026 Host Cities

The FIFA World Cup 2026 will take place across multiple cities in the United States, Canada, and Mexico. Major host cities include New York, Los Angeles, Dallas, Miami, Atlanta, Boston, Toronto, Vancouver, Mexico City, Guadalajara, and Monterrey.

Travel costs can vary significantly depending on which host city you choose to attend. Flights, hotel prices, and local transportation costs may be higher in large metropolitan areas compared to smaller host cities.

Average World Cup Travel Budget

  • Flights: $300 – $1,500 depending on distance
  • Hotels: $150 – $400 per night during the tournament
  • Match tickets: $150 – $600 depending on the stage
  • Food and local transport: $50 – $120 per day
